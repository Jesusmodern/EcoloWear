Welcome, on EcoloWear

Ecological

Our vegetal fabrics guarantee you comfort as well as superior quality. Remember, our main objective is the preservation of resources and the climate, respect for animal welfare, reduction of air pollution, but also water pollution. The production of electricity from our movements is therefore really the future of our planet.

Electricity

As explained previously, this electricity production is really the future of our planet because it initially allows a certain respect for the ecology. But also because from now on you can charge any device regardless of the movements you make.

Movement

You can now play sports while recharging your smartphone or simply take a walk in the city or in the forest. Ecolowear accompanies you everywhere no matter what activity you do, every movement is a dodge to nuclear power plants that are killing our planet little by little.

Vogue

We have therefore sought to absolutely respect sustainable development through the EcoloWear project. However, in addition to responsibility, EcoloWear is in search of authenticity and design is one of our main considerations.
